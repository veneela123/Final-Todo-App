About TodoApp

TodoApp is a simple and effective task management tool built using Spring Boot and Thymeleaf.

You can add, edit, delete, and track tasks effortlessly. The Task Tracker provides a clear view of your progress to help you stay motivated and organized.

Whether you're managing personal goals or daily chores, TodoApp helps you stay productive and focused.
